Meet Our Founder

Hi, I'm Sarah Amery. As a mother of three, I spent years navigating the chaotic world of children's birthday parties. I found myself choosing between "cheap and plastic" or "overwhelmingly DIY."

I started Amery Party Packs with a single mission: to provide parents with a curated, affordable luxury alternative. I believe you shouldn't have to break the bank to give your children a magical experience.

My goal is to take the stress out of planning while keeping the magic alive. We handle the aesthetics and the safety, so you can focus on making memories with your little ones.

Today, we are a small but mighty team of party enthusiasts, designers, and safety experts dedicated to delivering the 'Amery Standard' of stress-free magic to every doorstep in the city.

Sarah Amery, Founder & CEO
